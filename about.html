Our Military Heritage

Representing the Nigerian Army with discipline and teamwork

Military Sports Program

Established in 2015 as part of the Nigerian Army's sports initiative, the Rukuba Barracks Spikers represent discipline, teamwork, and excellence.

Our team consists of serving military personnel who balance duties with competitive volleyball. We maintain high standards of fitness and sportsmanship.

We represented Plateau State in national military sports competitions, winning the Armed Forces Games volleyball championship in 2019 and 2021.
