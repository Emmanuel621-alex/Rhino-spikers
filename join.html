Frequently Asked Questions

What are the practice schedules?

We practice three times a week: Mondays and Wednesdays 6-8pm, and Saturdays 9am-11am. Optional conditioning on Fridays.

What's the cost to join the team?

The annual membership fee is $1200, covering coaching, facility rentals, tournament fees, and team equipment. Payment plans available.

What's the commitment level expected?

Players are expected to attend at least 80% of practices and all scheduled tournaments. Dedication is essential.
